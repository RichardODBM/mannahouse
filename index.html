Welcome to Manna Hub

Just as manna was given freely each day to nourish and sustain, this hub offers a daily supply of Bible resources for you and your community. All materials here are provided without cost, so everyone can access God’s Word and tools for growth.

ODBM does not own these resources. They are gathered from around the world, made available by their creators, and shared freely for your use.

May you find spiritual nourishment here, just as the manna in the wilderness fed God’s people each morning.
